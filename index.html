The Nation of Nara

Nara is a meritocratic and feudalistic shogunate located in the +,+ on the continent of Alenarith. History has redefined Nara into a multicultural empire with regions of different styles and cultures joined under one Shogunate.

Founded on June 2nd, 2022, we've grown from a small settlement to a thriving nation with multiple cities, a robust economy, and a rich culture. Our philosophy is to chill and enjoy being part of a large and vibrant community from across the world.

Our Cities

Shiroyama

The capital and largest city of Nara. A world-class city featuring feudal and modern Japanese architecture with high density and environmental integration. Home to extensive security, production, and transport infrastructure.

Ichinokawa

A serene rural village featuring feudal Japanese architecture. An escape from the hustle and bustle of the big city with realistic farms, a tea shop, and a Shinto shrine dedicated to Tsukuyomi.

Q'Barra

An overseas exclave blending Aztec and Argonian fantasy themes. A magical place lost in the jungle with low density, high environmentalism, deep lore, and step pyramid temples.

Tronjheim

An independent province and breathtaking dwarven-themed underground city. Once a separate nation closely allied to Nara, Dalgon formally joined as an independent province. A huge underground haven for all dwarves.

Orakuru

A vibrant 1800s European industrial era city. Originally developed as an industrial area, now blending its origins with urban design, artistic and cultural expression with high density in mind.

Karasu

A welcoming settlement founded west of Ichinokawa. Originally settled by refugees from the Papal States, it has grown with a significant number of newfriends joining the community.

Our Culture

Nara was born as a nation inspired by East Asian styles, particularly Japanese. As we grew, we developed new styles inspired by different cultures, molding regions into different themes—from traditional feudal Japan to Aztec-Argonian fantasy, to dwarven underground cities.

Chill Philosophy

We aim for low drama and minding our own business, while still being a player on the international stage. We don't seek war but rather harmony.

Environmentalism

We carefully consider terrain in all construction, avoiding unnecessary destruction of natural features. Our builds integrate with the environment.

Multicultural Community

A vibrant community from across the world, with diverse architectural styles and cultures united under one Shogunate.
